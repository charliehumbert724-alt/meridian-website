5 things that actually move you up Google

There's a lot of noise about SEO. Most of it doesn't matter for a growing brand. Here are the five fundamentals that genuinely move the needle — in plain English.

1. Be fast and work on phones

Google ranks pages it can serve quickly, and most of your visitors are on a phone. A fast, mobile-friendly site is table stakes — get this wrong and nothing else matters.

2. Use the words your customers use

Write your page titles and headings the way real people search. "Affordable web design in Leeds" beats "Bespoke digital solutions" every time.

3. Give every page a clear job

One page, one purpose, one main keyword. Pages that try to be about everything rank for nothing.

4. Earn a few good links

A handful of mentions from trusted local or industry sites is worth more than a hundred spammy ones. Quality over quantity, always.

5. Keep publishing useful stuff

Fresh, genuinely helpful content tells Google you're active and worth ranking. A post like this one counts.

The best SEO strategy is being the most genuinely useful result for a real question someone is typing.
